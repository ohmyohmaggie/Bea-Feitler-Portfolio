Miss Holo

Bea Feitler was the art director of Ms. Magazine from 1972 to 1978. She was responsible for the magazine's iconic covers, which often featured bold typography and bright colors. This cover, from the December 1972 issue, is a perfect example of her style. The cover features neon green typography on top of a bright pink background, with the words "Peace on Earth, Good Will to People" in large, bold letters. The cover is a striking example of Feitler's use of color and typography to create eye-catching designs that draw the reader in.
